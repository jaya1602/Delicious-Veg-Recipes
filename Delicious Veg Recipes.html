Delicious Paneer Tikka
Ingredients:
  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice
Instructions:
  1. Mix yogurt with spices and lemon juice.
  2. Marinate paneer cubes for 30 mins.
  3. Grill until golden brown on both sides.
Healthy Green Salad
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Lettuce
  • 1/2 cup Cucumber (sliced)
  • 1 Tomato (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Olive Oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Combine all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Drizzle olive oil and season.
  3. Toss and serve fresh.
Spicy Dal Tadka
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Yellow Lentils
  • 1 Onion (chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 tbsp Ghee
Instructions:
  1. Boil dal until soft.
  2. Prepare tadka with ghee, cumin, and onions.
  3. Add tomatoes and pour over dal.
  4. Serve hot with rice or roti.
